Frequently Asked Questions

How do I add the widget to my home screen?

Touch and hold an empty area on your home screen until the apps jiggle, tap the + button in the top corner, search for "Word Widget", choose a widget size, and tap Add Widget.

The widget is not updating. What can I do?

Widgets are refreshed periodically by iOS. If the widget seems stuck, try opening the app once, or remove the widget from your home screen and add it again. Also make sure Background App Refresh is enabled in Settings > General > Background App Refresh.

How do I restore my purchases?

Open the app, go to Settings, and tap Restore Purchases. Make sure you are signed in with the same Apple ID you used for the original purchase.

How do I manage or cancel my subscription?

Subscriptions are managed by Apple. Open Settings > Your Name > Subscriptions on your device, or visit apps.apple.com/account/subscriptions to view, change, or cancel your subscription.

How do I request deletion of my data?

Send us an email at support@wordwidget.app from the address associated with your account and we will delete your data in accordance with our Privacy Policy.
